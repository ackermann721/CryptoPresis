Overview

CryptoPresis is a collection of 1821 algorithmically generated art pieces inspired by the President of El Salvador, Nayib Bukele. The first NFT project made by El Salvador for the world. The collection focuses on furthering education on crypto throughout El Salvador and donating to charitable organization such as Bitcoin Beach to increase further outreach and introduce our people and inspire other artists to the world of NFTs.

When did this project came to life?

The artist started with the first sketches in early September 2021. It took him quite some time to finalize all the attributes and determining the rarity of the individual attributes.

What is the total supply?

A total of 1821 unique CryptoPresis NFTs will be created. The total supply of the NFTs was inspired by the year of independence of El Salvador.
