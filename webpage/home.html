Overview

CryptoPresis is a collection of 1821 algorithmically generated art pieces inspired by the President of El Salvador, Nayib Bukele. The first NFT project made by El Salvador for the world. The collection focuses on furthering education on crypto throughout El Salvador and donating to charitable organization such as Bitcoin Beach to increase further outreach and introduce our people and inspire other artists to the world of NFTs.

Our Story

We're a team of 3 Salvadorans & 2 Honduran developers whom all share a deep passion for the world of Crypto and NFTs and meet through a similar NFT community. @Capitán Capibara / Meme Minister has been a project manager for over 15 years and runs a brick & mortar business in El Salvador, so he intends to his expertise learned over the years to manage this very transparent and community driven NFT project. @PxlPlnktn, our artist for this project, has a background in finance working for major financial institution in El Salvador but sought a more rewarding career in crypto as a very talented illustrator and we are happy that he is able to expand his presence further beyond the country's borders. @ackermann.eth is the lead blockchain developing with extensive experience in the field and will ensure a smooth and secure launch of this project on the Ethereum Blockchain. @pirateKing is our web developer with ample experience in full-stack development. Lastly, @migmarca also with a background in finance brings great value to the project as a marketing lead and head of moderation for our Discord server.
Our goal as creators is to provide as much value as we can for our holders. We’re not millionaires, we’re not mega flippers and we don’t have vast teams of influencers and big names at our disposal. We’re just 5 ordinary folks from third-world countries who’ve managed to stumble upon something truly remarkable.

F. A. Q.

When did this project came to life?

The artist started with the first sketches in early September 2021. It took him quite some time to finalize all the attributes and determining the rarity of the individual attributes.

What is the total supply?

A total of 1821 unique CryptoPresis NFTs will be created. The total supply of the NFTs was inspired by the year of independence of El Salvador.
