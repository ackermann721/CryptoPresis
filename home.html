Overview

CryptoPresis is a collection of 1821 algorithmically generated art pieces inspired by the President of El Salvador, Nayib Bukele. The first NFT project made by El Salvador for the world. The collection focuses on furthering education on crypto throughout El Salvador and donating to charitable organization such as Bitcoin Beach to increase further outreach, inspires and introduce local artists to the world of NFTs.
